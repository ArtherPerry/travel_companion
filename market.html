Targeting

Solo Travelers (25–35)

  • Traits: Tech-savvy, Budget-conscious, Adventurous
  • Needs: Local adventures, Meet people, Fast help, Safety, Freedom
  • Benefits: Real-time updates, personal ideas, smart 24/7 help

Business Travelers (30–50)

  • Traits: Frequent flyers, Efficiency-driven
  • Needs: Quick check-in, Time-saving, Plan adjustments
  • Benefits: Contactless check-in, smart room control, seamless assistance

Positioning

  • Smart help for travelers
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Contactless check-ins and payments
  • 24/7 support
  • Powered by leading-edge technology
